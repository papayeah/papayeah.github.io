"Be a 'lowercase d' designer."


It's been a year. One year mincing two questions—whether I should specialize or generalize, and striking a balance between writing/image. The feedback from four particular interviews, hours of conversation with mentors, and months of reflection alongside my peers has landed me here.

I'm still a specialist at heart—I have a lot of fun doing a few things well. Yet there are other skills and experiences beyond pushing pixels which demand our intention. Maybe these balancing questions are flawed. Maybe we've been both all along.

What do you think? You're here, so we're in touch somehow. Let's talk about it, and pull each other's hair out in the process.
